# **App Name**: GCSE Science Ace

## Core Features:

- Personalized Revision Plan: Generates a revision plan tailored to the user's weak areas, focusing on the AQA GCSE Triple Higher syllabus.
- Interactive Quizzes: Offers interactive quizzes with instant feedback. Questions adapt based on student performance to reinforce learning.
- AI Question Generation: Generates practice questions and prompts based on user performance. LLM as tool may decide that the student is not benefiting from a given line of questioning and move onto another topic.
- Flashcard Generator: Creates digital flashcards from key concepts in the revision plan. Allows users to customize the flashcards with their notes.
- Curated Reading: Suggests relevant AQA-approved reading material to supplement revision. Provides short summaries of these resources to help choose whats most impactful for revision.
- Progress Tracking: Tracks revision progress and quiz performance. Displays progress using charts and graphs to visualize learning.
- Comprehensive Content: Presents all required information from the AQA GCSE Triple Higher syllabus in a structured and easily digestible format.
- Video Resources: Links to freesciencelessons and Cognito videos for specific topics.
- Topic Summaries: Provides topic summaries at the click of a button, potentially AI generated.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep green (#006400) to evoke growth and learning.
- Background color: Off-white (#F5F5F5) for a clean and focused environment.
- Accent color: Bright lime green (#32CD32) to highlight important sections and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' for clear readability, a grotesque-style sans-serif.
- Use clear, simple icons to represent different topics in science and revision activities.
- Subtle animations provide feedback and keep the user engaged.
